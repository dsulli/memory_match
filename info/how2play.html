How to Play

League of Matching plays like a standard memory match game: flip over two cards at a time and try to match them each turn. The unique part of this game is that you have a health bar:

1000/1000

You start out at 1000 HP and lose 100 HP on each turn you mismatch cards. If you reach 0 HP, it's game over. However, you can buy items to help you. You start out with 300 gold, and can earn 300 more gold each time you make a match.

300

Items can boost your health, reduce the HP you lose on mismatch, help you earn gold, and even give you a chance to make a match on the first card you flip! You can view the description of what each item does by hovering over the item icons or by clicking on the items link at the top of the page. The gold cost of the item is right below the item's icon.

300

Ancient Coin 300 Passive: Inventory item, effect permanent. Gain +30 gold on mismatch.
